Important Consumer Warning

Payday loans are high-cost loans that can trap borrowers in cycles of debt. The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau reports that most payday borrowers remain in debt for 5 months of the year. Before taking a payday loan, consider alternatives like credit union loans, payment plans with creditors, or community assistance programs.
