üìä Understanding APR (Annual Percentage Rate)

What is APR?

APR is the yearly cost of a loan, including interest and fees, expressed as a percentage. It helps you compare the true cost of different loans.

How Payday Loan APR is Calculated

Example: $100 loan with $15 fee for 14 days
Step 1: Cost of credit = $15
Step 2: Cost √∑ Principal = $15 √∑ $100 = 0.15 (15%)
Step 3: Annualize = 0.15 √ó (365 √∑ 14) = 3.91 (391%)
Result: 391% APR
